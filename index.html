About Candy Chaos

Your kingdom just got invaded by candy monsters! It's time for you, the candy fairy to let your beautiful wings out and save the kingdom.

With total of three levels of monsters and three health only. Try your best to shoot all the monsters with that stick and win the game!

Play Now

Instructions

Controls

Use up, down, left and right arrow to move to the directions that you want. To shoot, click spacebar.

Game Mechanics

You will be given three health for each level, if you touched any monster, the health will be reduced by one. To go to next level, you must search and defeat all the monsters yourself.

Follow The Flow!

Don't you worry, just follow the storyline from level to level and enjoy every moment peacefully!
